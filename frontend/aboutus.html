About us

Welcome to TweetTruth! At the heart of our mission is a dedication to combating the spread of misinformation on one of the world's most influential social media platforms.

Leveraging the power of Natural Language Processing and deep learning, we harnessed the capabilities of LIWC (Linguistic Inquiry and Word Count) and PyTorch to create a robust tool for identifying and flagging fake news on Twitter.

Our project is the result of the collaborative efforts of a group of five passionate students from the University of New South Wales (UNSW). With a shared commitment to promoting accurate and reliable information, we have worked tirelessly to develop a cutting-edge solution that contributes to a more informed and discerning online community. Thank you for joining us in our quest to combat fake news and promote digital integrity. Together, we can make the internet a safer and more reliable space for all.
